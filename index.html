ABOUT EMMA

Born April 15, 1990 • Paris, France

EARLY LIFE

Born in Paris on April 15, 1990, Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson moved to England as a young child where her journey to stardom began. Her passion for acting was evident from an early age, landing her first major role at just nine years old. Even as a child, Emma demonstrated the determination and intelligence that would later define her career both on and off screen.

Her early education took place at the Dragon School in Oxford, where she excelled academically while pursuing acting. This balance of education and creative pursuits would become a hallmark of Emma's approach to life.

Child Actress Oxford Education Multi-talented

EDUCATION

Despite her busy filming schedule, Emma prioritized her education, demonstrating her commitment to intellectual growth alongside her professional career. After completing secondary school with high marks, she attended Brown University in the United States.

At Brown, Emma pursued a bachelor's degree in English Literature, graduating in 2014. Her academic journey showcases her belief that education empowers women to achieve their full potential, regardless of their career path. During her time at university, she continued to balance her studies with acting roles, proving that women need not choose between intellectual pursuits and professional success.

"I DON'T WANT OTHER PEOPLE TO DECIDE WHO I AM. I WANT TO DECIDE THAT FOR MYSELF."

UN WOMEN GOODWILL AMBASSADOR

In July 2014, Emma Watson was appointed as a UN Women Goodwill Ambassador, marking a significant milestone in her advocacy work. In this role, she has dedicated herself to empowering young women and advocating for gender equality worldwide.

Her appointment led to the launch of the groundbreaking HeForShe campaign, which invites people of all genders to stand in solidarity with women and girls as equal partners in the craft of positive change. Her iconic speech at the UN Headquarters in New York challenged traditional gender roles and called for men to become advocates for gender equality.

"GENDER EQUALITY IS YOUR ISSUE TOO."
HeForShe Campaign UN Speech Global Advocate

ACTIVISM

HEFORSHE CAMPAIGN

Launched in 2014, the HeForShe campaign represents Emma's most significant contribution to the gender equality movement. As the face of this revolutionary initiative, she has traveled globally to raise awareness and gather support for women's rights issues.

The campaign specifically calls on men and boys to act as agents of change by taking action against the inequalities and discrimination faced by women and girls. This inclusive approach recognizes that gender equality benefits everyone, not just women.

"HOW CAN WE EFFECT CHANGE IN THE WORLD WHEN ONLY HALF OF IT IS INVITED OR FEEL WELCOME TO PARTICIPATE IN THE CONVERSATION?"

FEMINIST BOOK CLUB

FEMINIST BOOK CLUB

In January 2016, Emma founded "Our Shared Shelf," a feminist book club aimed at sharing ideas and encouraging discussion about gender equality through literature. The online community quickly grew, bringing together readers from around the world.

Each month, Emma selected books that explore feminist themes and issues, using literature as a powerful tool to educate and inspire. Through this initiative, she has created a space for open dialogue and learning about feminism, intersectionality, and women's experiences globally.

"BOOKS GAVE ME A WAY TO CONNECT WITH MY FATHER. SOME OF MY MOST PRECIOUS AND TREASURED MOMENTS..."
Literary Activism Global Community Educational Initiative

WOMEN'S RIGHTS ADVOCACY

Beyond her formal initiatives, Emma has been a vocal advocate for women's rights in various spheres. She has spoken out on issues ranging from campus sexual assault to reproductive rights, using her platform to amplify important causes.

In 2018, Emma donated £1 million to the UK Justice and Equality Fund, which aims to combat sexual harassment, assault, and discrimination. Her contributions extend beyond financial support, as she regularly participates in demonstrations, signs open letters, and engages with grassroots organizations.

"FEMINISM IS NOT A STICK WITH WHICH TO BEAT OTHER WOMEN WITH. IT'S ABOUT FREEDOM, IT'S ABOUT LIBERATION, IT'S ABOUT EQUALITY."

HARRY POTTER FRANCHISE

Emma Watson's breakthrough role as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter film series catapulted her to international fame. Over the course of a decade, she portrayed the intelligent, brave, and resourceful witch in all eight films, becoming an iconic role model for young girls worldwide.

Hermione's character challenged stereotypes and showcased a young woman whose intelligence and courage were central to the story. Through this character, Emma began her journey of representing strong female characters on screen, a pattern that would continue throughout her career.

"YOUNG GIRLS ARE TOLD YOU HAVE TO BE THE DELICATE PRINCESS. HERMIONE TAUGHT THEM THAT YOU CAN BE THE WARRIOR."
Global Recognition Box Office Success Role Model
2012-PRESENT

DIVERSE FILM ROLES

Following the conclusion of the Harry Potter series, Emma successfully transitioned to more diverse and mature roles, demonstrating her range and talent as an actress. Films like "The Perks of Being a Wallflower," "The Bling Ring," and "Little Women" showcased her ability to portray complex, multidimensional female characters.

Her role as Belle in Disney's live-action "Beauty and the Beast" (2017) reimagined the classic princess as an inventor and active character, reflecting Emma's commitment to portraying strong women on screen. Throughout her post-Potter career, she has consistently chosen roles that challenge stereotypes and present women as fully realized individuals.

SUSTAINABLE FASHION

Emma has established herself as a style icon who champions ethical and sustainable fashion. As the face of Burberry and collaborating with designers like Alberta Ferretti, she uses her influence in the fashion industry to promote environmentally conscious practices.

In 2018, she launched the Instagram account @the_press_tour to document her sustainable fashion choices during press events, educating followers about eco-friendly brands and practices. Her dedication to ethical fashion demonstrates how women can use their platform and personal choices to advocate for important causes.

"FASHION IS A GREAT WAY TO EMPOWER PEOPLE AND GIVE THEM SKILLS."
